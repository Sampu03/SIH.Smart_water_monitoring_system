Medicine Suggestions

Cholera Medications

Oral Rehydration Solution (ORS) - Prevents dehydration
Azithromycin - Antibiotic for severe cases
Doxycycline - Alternative antibiotic
Zinc supplements - For children to reduce duration

Typhoid Medications

Ciprofloxacin - First-line antibiotic treatment
Ceftriaxone - For resistant cases
Azithromycin - Alternative antibiotic
Paracetamol - For fever management

Hepatitis A Medications

Rest and hydration - Primary treatment
Paracetamol - For fever (avoid alcohol)
Anti-nausea drugs - If vomiting persists
Vitamin supplements - To support recovery

Acute Diarrhea Medications

Oral Rehydration Solution (ORS) - Essential for all cases
Zinc supplements - Reduces duration and severity
Loperamide - For adults only (short-term use)
Probiotics - Helps restore gut flora

Dysentery Medications

Metronidazole - For amoebic dysentery
Ciprofloxacin - For bacterial dysentery
ORS and Zinc - Prevent dehydration
Paracetamol - For fever and pain
